Powerful adversaries throughout the Lands Between. From lowly field bosses to the mighty Demigods themselves, each presents a unique challenge for the Tarnished.

Boss Categories

Bosses are distinguished by the message displayed upon defeat. Unlike regular enemies, bosses do not respawn after being slain. Some are required for progression while most are optional.

Demigod – Shardbearers with Great Runes
Legend – Powerful story bosses
Great Enemy – Mini-bosses, gate guardians
Field Boss – Open world encounters
Dungeon Boss – Caves, catacombs, tunnels
Dragon – Ancient dragons and wyrms
DLC – Shadow of the Erdtree

Demigods & Shardbearers

The children of Marika who bear shards of the Elden Ring. Defeating them grants Great Runes and Remembrances.

Demigod

Godrick the Grafted

Shardbearer
The weakest of the demigods, Godrick grafts the limbs of his victims onto himself in pursuit of power he was never born with.
"A knower of grafting, who has made the bodies of heroes his own, and the first king among the Tarnished."
Great Rune Optional Fire Phase 2
Stormveil Castle 20,000
Demigod

Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon

Shardbearer
Head of the Carian Royal Family and leader of the Academy of Raya Lucaria. A powerful sorceress who wields the full moon's magic.
"Rennala is Queen of the Carian Royal Family, and the Academy's matriarch. Her Great Rune is the amber egg she cradles."
Great Rune Optional Sorcery
Raya Lucaria Academy 40,000
Demigod

Starscourge Radahn

Shardbearer
The mightiest of the demigods, General Radahn conquered the stars themselves. Now he rots in Caelid, mindlessly feasting on corpses.
"The Red Lion General, once the mightiest demigod of the Shattering, reduced to mindless wandering."
Great Rune Required* Festival
Redmane Castle, Caelid 70,000

Shardbearer
The true identity of Margit. Despite being an Omen, cursed from birth, Morgott became the last king to defend Leyndell.
"Though born an Omen, Morgott remained devoted to the Erdtree, earning his place as the last king."

Leyndell, Royal Capital 120,000
Demigod

Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy

Shardbearer
Rykard fed himself to the God-Devouring Serpent to gain power. Together, they plan to consume all the gods of the Lands Between.
"In the belly of the beast, family will be all the more, TOGETHAAAA!"

Volcano Manor 130,000
Demigod

Mohg, Lord of Blood

Shardbearer
Morgott's twin brother, also born an Omen. Mohg kidnapped Miquella and founded the Mohgwyn Dynasty, seeking to raise a new god.

Shardbearer

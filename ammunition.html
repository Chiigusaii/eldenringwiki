AMMUNITION

Arrows and bolts for bows, crossbows, and ballistae

◆ ◆ ◆

About Ammunition

Arrows and Bolts are ammunition for ranged weapons. Arrows are used with Bows and Light Bows, Bolts with Crossbows, Great Arrows with Greatbows, and Greatbolts with Ballistae. Arrows add their damage to your bow's base AR and scale with the bow's stats, while bolts and crossbows have no scaling.

Status effects from ammunition (poison, bleed, sleep) scale with Arcane when using the Serpent Bow. Fire and Holy damage scale with Faith on the Erdtree Bow, while Magic damage scales with Intelligence on the Horn Bow.

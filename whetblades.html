About Whetblades

Whetblades are key items that expand your weapon customization options by unlocking additional affinities for Ashes of War. When you apply an Ash of War to a weapon at a Site of Grace, you can choose different affinities that modify the weapon's scaling and damage types.

How They Work:

  • Each Whetblade unlocks specific affinity options (e.g., Heavy, Keen, Quality, Magic, etc.)
  • Once acquired, a Whetblade's effects are permanent and apply to all compatible weapons
  • Not all Ashes of War can use all affinitiesâ€”some are restricted by weapon type
  • The Whetstone Knife (given by Smithing Master Hewg) is required to apply Ashes of War at Sites of Grace

Whetstone Knife

Base Tool
Tool given by Smithing Master Hewg that enables you to apply Ashes of War at Sites of Grace. Required before any other Whetblades can be used.
"A tool used for weapon reinforcement. Provides basic functionality for applying Ashes of War, allowing warriors to customize their armaments to match their fighting style."
Base Affinity
Standard
Location
Given by Smithing Master Hewg at the Roundtable Hold after resting at a Site of Grace in Limgrave (or immediately if arriving at Roundtable Hold before visiting Limgrave).
Base Game
How to Obtain
  • Speak to Smithing Master Hewg at the Roundtable Hold
  • Must have rested at any Site of Grace in Limgrave first
  • Automatically given when you meet the requirements
  • Cannot be missed as it's essential for game progression
What Standard Affinity Does

Standard affinity keeps the weapon's original scaling and damage distribution. This is the default option when applying any Ash of War.

Iron Whetblade

Physical Affinities
A whetstone that unlocks physical affinities for Ashes of War. Allows you to choose Heavy, Keen, or Quality affinities when applying Ashes of War.
"An ordinary whetblade used for common weapon reinforcement. Provides fundamental options for physical scaling: Heavy for strength, Keen for dexterity, or Quality for balanced builds."
Unlocked Affinities
Heavy (STR) Keen (DEX) Quality (STR/DEX)
Location
Stormveil Castle: In a small room on the southeastern side of the castle, accessed after going through the castle ramparts. Near the Rampart Tower Site of Grace.
Base Game
Detailed Location Guide
  • From Rampart Tower Site of Grace, head southeast along the castle walls
  • Look for a small room with wooden scaffolding
  • The whetblade is in a chest inside this room
  • Watch out for the Exile Soldiers patrolling nearby
Affinity Comparison
Affinity Best For Scaling
Heavy Pure STR builds (60+ STR) High STR, Low DEX
Keen Pure DEX builds (60+ DEX) High DEX, Low STR
Quality Balanced builds (40/40) Equal STR/DEX
ðŸ’¡

Tip: Heavy and Keen typically outdamage Quality at high stat investments (60+), but Quality shines in the 40/40 range.

Recommended Weapons

Heavy: Greatswords, Colossal Weapons, Great Hammers

Keen: Katanas, Curved Swords, Daggers, Thrusting Swords

Quality: Straight Swords, Spears, most versatile weapons

Glintstone Whetblade

Magic Affinities
A whetstone forged from glintstone that unlocks magic affinities. Allows Magic and Cold affinities when applying Ashes of War.
"Whetblade of blue-white stone found in the land of Liurnia. Said to have been used by the Carian royal family's astrologers. Allows sorcerous imbuement of armaments."
Unlocked Affinities
Magic (INT) Cold (INT + Frostbite)
Location
Raya Lucaria Academy: In a treasure chest on the upper level of the Debate Parlor, accessible after defeating the Red Wolf of Radagon. Look for a western balcony area.
Base Game
How to Reach
  • Progress through Raya Lucaria Academy until you reach the Debate Parlor
  • Defeat the Red Wolf of Radagon boss
  • From the Debate Parlor Site of Grace, take the lift up
  • Exit to the western balcony and find the chest
Magic vs Cold
Affinity Effect Best Use
Magic Adds magic damage Pure INT builds for maximum damage
Cold Adds magic damage + frostbite Builds wanting status effect utility

Tip: Frostbite lowers enemy defenses by 20% for 30 seconds, making Cold excellent for sustained fights!

Best Weapons

Magic: Fast weapons that benefit from split scaling (Twin Blades, Curved Swords)

Cold: Weapons with fast hit rates to proc frostbite (Dual-wielded weapons, Flails)

Red-Hot Whetblade

Fire Affinities
A whetstone smelted in volcanic flame that unlocks fire affinities. Allows Fire Art and Flame Art affinities when applying Ashes of War.
"Whetblade born of magma that seeped from the peak of Mt. Gelmir. Allows elemental affinity infusion. The flames born of sorcery differ from ordinary flames."
Unlocked Affinities
Fire Art (STR/DEX) Flame Art (FAI)
Location
Redmane Castle: In a treasure chest at the top of the southwestern tower. Accessible from the chamber of the Misbegotten Warrior and Crucible Knight boss fight.
Base Game
Location Guide
  • Travel to Redmane Castle in southeastern Caelid
  • Defeat the Misbegotten Warrior and Crucible Knight duo boss
  • From the boss chamber, find the exit to the southwestern tower
  • Climb to the top of the tower to find the chest

Sanctified Whetblade

Lightning & Holy Affinities
A whetstone blessed by ancient lightning and the Golden Order. Unlocks Lightning and Sacred affinities for Ashes of War.
"A sacred whetblade, misplaced by Leyndell knights. Allows both the thunderous power of ancient dragons and the blessed might of the Erdtree to be imbued into armaments."
Unlocked Affinities
Lightning (DEX) Sacred (FAI)
Location
Leyndell, Royal Capital: In the Fortified Manor, First Floor. From the West Capital Rampart Site of Grace, enter through the main entrance. The whetblade is on a corpse in a room to the northeast.
Base Game
Detailed Directions
  • Reach Leyndell, Royal Capital (requires 2 Great Runes)
  • Fast travel to West Capital Rampart Site of Grace
  • Enter the Fortified Manor through the main entrance
  • Navigate to the northeastern room on the first floor
  • Find the whetblade on a corpse near a table

Black Whetblade

Status Effect Affinities
A mysterious black whetstone that unlocks status effect affinities. Allows Poison, Blood, and Occult affinities when applying Ashes of War.
"A whetblade as black as the night. Opens up affinities that were sealed away by the Golden Order. Once upon a time, in the black home of the Nox, weapons were smithed that clashed with the Order."
Unlocked Affinities
Poison (STR/DEX/ARC) Blood (ARC) Occult (ARC)
Location
Nokron, Eternal City: In the Night's Sacred Ground, past the Mimic Tear boss arena. Found on a corpse near the edge of a cliff overlooking the city, behind a large stone monument.
Base Game
How to Access Nokron
  • Defeat Starscourge Radahn in Redmane Castle
  • A meteor will crash near Fort Haight in Mistwood
  • Enter the crater to access Nokron, Eternal City
  • Progress through to defeat the Mimic Tear boss
  • Continue past the boss arena to Night's Sacred Ground
  • Look for a corpse near the cliff edge behind the monument

Status Build Strategy

Poison: Good for passive damage over time, less viable in PvP

Blood: Excellent burst damage, dominant in both PvE and PvP

Occult: Best for pure Arcane scaling without status effects, boosts item discovery

Affinity Selection Guide

Physical Builds:

  • Heavy - Best for pure Strength builds (STR 60+)
  • Keen - Best for pure Dexterity builds (DEX 60+)
  • Quality - Best for balanced STR/DEX builds (both 40+)

Magic Builds:

  • Magic - Adds magic damage, scales with Intelligence
  • Cold - Adds magic damage + Frostbite buildup, scales with Intelligence

Fire Builds:

  • Fire Art - Adds fire damage, scales with Strength/Dexterity (split scaling)
  • Flame Art - Adds fire damage, scales with Faith

Faith Builds:

  • Lightning - Adds lightning damage, scales with Dexterity (some Faith)
  • Sacred - Adds holy damage, scales with Faith

Arcane Builds:

  • Poison - Poison buildup, scales with STR/DEX/ARC
  • Blood - Blood loss buildup, scales with Arcane
  • Occult - Increases base damage, scales with Arcane (no status effect)

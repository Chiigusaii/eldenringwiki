GREAT RUNES

◆ ✧ ◆

Powerful shards of the Elden Ring, inherited by the demigod offspring of Queen Marika following the Shattering. These Great Runes grant immense power when activated at Divine Towers and used with a Rune Arc.

How to Use Great Runes

1

Defeat a Shardbearer

Obtain a Great Rune by defeating one of the demigod bosses throughout the Lands Between.

2

Activate at Divine Tower

Travel to the corresponding Divine Tower and restore the Great Rune's power at the top.

3

Equip the Rune

Rest at a Site of Grace and equip your chosen Great Rune from the Great Runes menu.

4

Use a Rune Arc

Consume a Rune Arc from your inventory to activate the Great Rune's effect until death.

Godrick's Great Rune

Godrick the Grafted
Effect
Raises all attributes by +5 points
"This Great Rune is known as the anchor ring, found in the center of the Elden Ring. The first demigods were The Elden Lord Godfrey and his offspring, the golden lineage."
Stormveil Castle, Limgrave Divine Tower of Limgrave
Best For: Early game flexibility. The +5 boost to all stats is ideal for meeting equipment and spell requirements across any build.

Great Rune of the Unborn

Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon
Effect
Cannot be equipped. Grants the ability to "Rebirth" (respec character stats) at Raya Lucaria Grand Library
"Amber egg clutched by Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon. Great Rune of unborn demigods. Perfects those who have been born anew. Children born anew by Rennala are all frail and short-lived."
Raya Lucaria Academy, Liurnia No Activation Required
Note: This Great Rune is passive and doesn't require activation. Requires a Larval Tear to respec with Rennala.

Radahn's Great Rune

Starscourge Radahn
Effect
Raises maximum HP by 15%, FP by 12.5%, and Stamina by 12.5%
"Radahn was amongst the children of Rennala and Radagon, who became demigod stepchildren after Radagon's union with Queen Marika. The Great Rune burns, to resist the encroachment of the scarlet rot."
Redmane Castle, Caelid Divine Tower of Caelid
Best For: All builds. The balanced increase to core resources (HP/FP/Stamina) benefits every playstyle reliably.

Morgott's Great Rune

Morgott, the Omen King
Effect
Greatly raises maximum HP by approximately 25%
"A Great Rune of the shardbearer Morgott. Its blessing greatly raises maximum HP. Morgott was born of the Golden Lineage, the line of Godfrey, first Elden Lord. He was the twin brother of Mohg."
Leyndell, Royal Capital Divine Tower of East Altus
Best For: Tank builds and survivability. The massive 25% HP boost stacks with all other HP increases for maximum durability.

Rykard's Great Rune

Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy
Effect
Restores HP upon defeating enemies (80 + 7% of max HP)
"Rykard was amongst the children of Rennala and Radagon, who became demigod stepchildren after Radagon's union with Queen Marika. But Rykard fed himself to the blasphemous serpent, Great Rune and all."
Volcano Manor, Mt. Gelmir Divine Tower of West Altus
Best For: Aggressive playstyles clearing groups. Effect only triggers on enemy kills, making it less useful for boss fights.

Mohg's Great Rune

Mohg, Lord of Blood
Effect (PvE)
Grants summoned spirits the Blessing of Blood. When your summon kills an enemy, you gain 10% of max HP. Blood loss near summons increases their attack power.
"Mohg, Lord of Blood's Great Rune is soaked with accursed blood. It's said that wounds festered with this blood will never heal. The mother of truth desires a wound."
Mohgwyn Dynasty Mausoleum Divine Tower of East Altus
Best For: Spirit Ash summon builds. Particularly powerful with Mimic Tear or bleed-focused summons like the Bloodhound Knight.

Malenia's Great Rune

Malenia, Blade of Miquella
Effect
Attacks after taking damage restore HP (like Bloodborne's Rally system). Reduces Flask of Crimson Tears healing to ~25% effectiveness.
"Malenia's Great Rune is half-rotted but infused with her spirit of resistance. She has never known defeat. The Aeonia blooms for the third time."
Elphael, Brace of the Haligtree Isolated Divine Tower
Best For: High-risk, high-reward aggressive builds. Dexterity and bleed builds that can quickly counter-attack. Flask healing penalty makes this very challenging.

Miquella's Great Rune

Shadow of the Erdtree DLC
Effect
Can only be activated during the Promised Consort Radahn boss fight. Removes the debuff applied by Radahn's grab attack.
"Miquella discarded his Great Rune upon entering the Land of Shadow. It shatters when crossing into the eastern half of Scadu Altus, Rauh Base region, or Shadow Keep."
Shadow of the Erdtree Limited Use
Note: This Great Rune is only usable in one specific boss fight and cannot be equipped like other Great Runes. Considered the weakest Great Rune due to its extremely limited application.

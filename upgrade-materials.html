UPGRADE MATERIALS

◆ ✧ ◆

Essential materials for strengthening your weapons, shields, spirit ashes, and sacred flasks. Smithing Stones reinforce armaments, Gloveworts empower spirit ashes, and special items enhance your flasks and blessings. Collect Bell Bearings to purchase unlimited quantities from the Twin Maiden Husks.

Upgrading Your Equipment

1

Collect Materials

Find upgrade materials throughout the Lands Between—in mines, catacombs, dropped by enemies, or purchased from merchants.

2

Visit a Smithing Table

Use any Smithing Table found at Sites of Grace or visit Master Hewg at Roundtable Hold for weapon upgrades.

3

Spirit Tuning

Speak with Roderika at Roundtable Hold to strengthen your Spirit Ashes using Grave and Ghost Gloveworts.

4

Bell Bearings

Collect Miner's Bell Bearings to unlock unlimited material purchases from the Twin Maiden Husks.

Total Upgrade Costs

Bell Bearing Tip

While materials can be scavenged throughout the Lands Between, collecting Bell Bearings and giving them to the Twin Maiden Husks allows you to purchase unlimited quantities. This makes upgrading multiple weapons much easier in the late game.

Gloveworts

White flowers used to strengthen Spirit Ashes. Grave Gloveworts for standard ashes, Ghost Gloveworts for renowned ashes.

Grave Glovewort (Standard Spirit Ashes)

Grave Glovewort [1]

Spirit Material
Strengthen ashes to +1

White flower that blooms in catacombs. Each a soundless bell used to summon spirits. The art of strengthening ashes is known as spirit tuning.

800 Runes Base Game

Grave Glovewort [2-9]

Spirit Material
Strengthen ashes to +2 through +9

Spirit tuning is not merely the simple act of imbuing strength. It is an art that lies somewhere between tuning an instrument and conducting a conversation.

1,200 - 8,000 Runes Base Game

Great Grave Glovewort

Legendary Material
Strengthen ashes to +10 (Maximum)

An especially large specimen. Since times of old, large gloveworts were used to comfort heroic spirits. Given in tribute to those who died the most glorious of deaths.

Cannot be purchased Base Game

Sacred Flask & Blessings

Materials to enhance your Sacred Flasks and the special blessings available in the Shadow of the Erdtree DLC.

Sacred Flask Upgrades

Golden Seed

Flask Enhancement
Increases Sacred Flask uses (up to 14 total)

A golden seed, found at the base of an illusory tree. When the Elden Ring was shattered, these seeds flew from the Erdtree, scattering across the various lands, as if life itself knew its end had come.

Found at Minor Erdtrees Base Game

Sacred Tear

Flask Enhancement
Improves Sacred Flask potency (up to +12)

Blessing of the Erdtree, worshiped in the churches of all lands; this is but a faint vestige. During the age of the Erdtree, these tears were used to spread the faith.

Found at Churches Base Game

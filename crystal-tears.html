CRYSTAL TEARS

◆ ✧ ◆

Mystical crystallized tears formed where the Erdtree's bounty falls to the ground. Mix two into your Flask of Wondrous Physick at any Site of Grace for powerful temporary effects. There are 32 base game tears and 8 DLC tears from Furnace Golems.

About Crystal Tears

Crystal Tears are Key Items that can be mixed into the Flask of Wondrous Physick. You can combine any two tears for unique buff combinations. The flask replenishes at Sites of Grace and all tears carry over to New Game+.

Most tears are obtained from Erdtree Avatars guarding Minor Erdtrees, stone offering bowls, or quest rewards. The 8 DLC tears are exclusively dropped by Furnace Golems in the Realm of Shadow.

Restoration Tears

Restore HP, FP, or stamina. Essential for survival in the Lands Between.

Bell Bearings

Key items that expand the Twin Maiden Husks' shop at the Roundtable Hold

◆ ◆ ◆

About Bell Bearings

Bell Bearings are Key Items which, when given to the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold, expand their shop with new items. Though often collected after the death of NPCs, these bearings are occasionally dropped by Bosses or found throughout the Lands Between.

In total, there are 62 Bell Bearings — 51 in the base game and 10 in Shadow of the Erdtree. Only certain Bell Bearings that add crafting or upgrade materials are kept when going into New Game+; those from NPCs or Merchants must be collected again.

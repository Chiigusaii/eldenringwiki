ITEMS

A comprehensive catalog of items found throughout the Lands Between and the Land of Shadow. Browse by category below or use the search to find specific items across all categories.

Consumables

100+ Items
Attack buffs, defense buffs, weapon greases, throwing pots, boluses for curing status effects, cured meats, raisins, and perfumes.
Fire Grease • Boiled Crab • Uplifting Aromatic

Crystal Tears

37 Items
Key items that can be mixed in the Flask of Wondrous Physick for various special effects and buffs. Found at Minor Erdtrees and Furnace Golems.
Cerulean Hidden Tear • Opaline Bubbletear • Flame-Shrouding

Great Runes

8 Items
Shards of the Elden Ring dropped by Shardbearer demigods. Must be activated at Divine Towers, then empowered with Rune Arcs.
Godrick's • Radahn's • Morgott's • Malenia's

Remembrances

25 Items
Boss souls that can be traded for unique weapons, spells, or consumed for runes. Duplicable at Walking Mausoleums.
Grafted • Starscourge • Rot Goddess • Impaler

Ammunition

40+ Items
Arrows, bolts, great arrows, and greatbolts for ranged combat. Arrow damage scales with bow; bolt damage is fixed.
Fire Arrow • Serpent Arrow • Radahn's Spear

Bell Bearings

30+ Items
Items that unlock new shop inventories at the Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable Hold. Includes smithing stones, cookbooks, and merchant wares.
Smithing-Stone Miner's • Meat Peddler's • Bone Peddler's

Cookbooks

40+ Items
Recipe books that unlock crafting recipes. Includes Nomadic Warrior's, Armorer's, Missionary's, Glintstone Craftsman's, and Perfumer's cookbooks.
Nomadic Warrior's [1-24] • Armorer's [1-7] • Perfumer's

Crafting Materials

80+ Items
Raw materials used for crafting consumables. Includes plants, minerals, animal parts, and enemy drops scattered throughout the world.
Rowa Fruit • Erdleaf Flower • Cave Moss • Silver Firefly

Key Items

60+ Items
Quest-related items, keys, medallions, and other important items needed for progression or accessing secret areas.
Dectus Medallion • Haligtree Medallion • Academy Key

Whetblades

6 Items
Key items that unlock additional weapon affinities when applying Ashes of War at Sites of Grace. Essential for build customization.
Iron • Glintstone • Red-Hot • Black Whetblade

Tools

40+ Items
Reusable utility items including lanterns, telescopes, Spirit Calling Bell, invasion items, and quest-related tools.
Lantern • Mimic's Veil • Margit's Shackle • Telescope

Upgrade Materials

30+ Items
Materials for upgrading weapons and Spirit Ashes. Includes Smithing Stones, Somber Smithing Stones, Gloveworts, and Ancient Dragon Smithing Stones.
Smithing Stone [1-9] • Somber [1-10] • Glovewort [1-9]

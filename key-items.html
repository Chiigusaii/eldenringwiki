KEY ITEMS

◆ ✧ ◆

Essential items that unlock areas, advance quests, increase character capabilities, and serve as containers for crafting. Many are unique per playthrough and cannot be dropped or traded.

About Key Items

Key Items are special items that serve various purposes—unlocking sealed doors, activating lifts, increasing spell slots or talisman slots, or providing containers for crafting. Most are unique and cannot be obtained again until New Game+.

Some key items like Stonesword Keys are consumable and require careful consideration before use. Others like Memory Stones provide permanent passive benefits once collected.

Keys & Access Items

Items that unlock sealed doors, imp statues, and waygates throughout the Lands Between.

Stonesword Key

Consumable Key
Breaks seal on imp statues (consumed on use)
Sword-shaped stone key. Unlocks fog doors guarded by imp statues. Can be chosen as a starting Keepsake. ~30 found per playthrough.
Consumed Various Merchants

Imbued Sword Key

Rare Key
Activates sending gates at The Four Belfries
Stone key imbued with rich blue magic. Only 4 exist per playthrough. Gates lead to Farum Azula, Nokron, and Chapel of Anticipation.
Consumed 4 Per Playthrough

Academy Glintstone Key

Major Key
Opens both gates to Raya Lucaria Academy
Activates warp magic bound within the seals. A glintstone key remembers its user—once used, it can never be passed to another.
Reusable Behind Glintstone Dragon

Rusty Key

Dungeon Key
Opens locked door in Stormveil Castle
Rusted iron key found in Stormveil. Opens the door to the area containing the Chrysalid's Memento.
Reusable Stormveil Castle

Drawing-Room Key

Manor Key
Opens Volcano Manor's drawing room
Key to Volcano Manor's drawing room, given by Tanith. The door can be found in the westward corridor, upon the left hand side.
Reusable Tanith Quest

Sewer-Gaol Key

Dungeon Key
Unlocks sewer-gaol door in Leyndell
Filthy key received from the Dung Eater. Unlocks his cell in the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds beneath Leyndell.
Reusable Dung Eater Quest

Discarded Palace Key

Hidden Key
Opens chest in Raya Lucaria Grand Library
Found after defeating Astel in Lake of Rot. Opens the chest containing the Dark Moon Ring for Ranni's questline.
Reusable Astel Drop

Sellian Sealbreaker

Magic Key
Breaks seal confining Master Lusat
Glintstone key received from Sellen. Capable of breaking the seal that keeps Master Lusat confined in Sellia Hideaway.
Reusable Sellen Quest

Medallions

Split medallions that activate the Grand Lifts, granting access to major regions.

Dectus Medallion (Left)

Split Medallion
Half of medallion for Grand Lift of Dectus
Left half depicting the Erdtree. Found in Fort Haight on the Weeping Peninsula coast. The right half is said to reside in Fort Faroth.
Reusable Fort Haight

Dectus Medallion (Right)

Split Medallion
Half of medallion for Grand Lift of Dectus
Right half depicting the Erdtree. Found in Fort Faroth in the Dragonbarrow. Connects Altus Plateau to Liurnia when conjoined.
Reusable Fort Faroth

Rold Medallion

Whole Medallion
Activates Grand Lift of Rold
Conjoined medallion depicting the Flame Peak. Given by Melina after defeating Morgott. Connects Leyndell to the Mountaintops of the Giants.
Reusable Story Item

Haligtree Secret Medallion (Left)

Secret Medallion
Half of secret medallion for hidden path
Grants access to hidden path at Grand Lift of Rold leading to Miquella's Haligtree. Found atop Castle Sol after defeating Commander Niall.
Reusable Castle Sol

Haligtree Secret Medallion (Right)

Secret Medallion
Half of secret medallion for hidden path
Given by Albus (disguised as pot) in Village of the Albinaurics. Guides the bearer to Miquella's Haligtree when conjoined.
Reusable Albus/Latenna

Progression Items

Items that permanently increase your character's capabilities—spell slots, talisman slots, and flask charges.

Memory Stone

Slot Increase
+1 Memory Slot for spells/incantations
Black, lightly beguiling stone. 8 can be found for a total of 10 slots (start with 2). Mostly found in Rises (magic towers) or boss drops.
Passive 8 Per Playthrough

Talisman Pouch

Slot Increase
+1 Talisman equip slot
Small withered bag. 3 can be found for 4 total slots. Obtained from Margit, Enia (after 2 Great Runes), and Godfrey (golden shade).
Passive Boss Rewards

Golden Seed

Flask Upgrade
+1 Flask charge at Sites of Grace
Golden seed found at foot of illusory trees. Maximum 14 flasks total. Found near Minor Erdtrees, church sites, and certain boss drops.
Consumed at Grace ~30+ Available

Sacred Tear

Flask Upgrade
Increases HP/FP restored per flask use
Tear from a statue of Marika. Found at churches throughout the Lands Between. Maximum +12 flask potency.
Consumed at Grace Churches

Larval Tear

Respec Item
Respec character stats at Rennala
Rare creature that burgeons from spiritgraves. Required to be born anew and reallocate attributes. ~18 per playthrough.
Consumed Limited Supply

Whetstone Knife

Feature Unlock
Enables Ash of War application
Whetstone with cipher inscription. Allows applying Ashes of War at Sites of Grace to bestow new skills and affinities to armaments.
Permanent Gatefront Ruins

Crafting Containers

Reusable vessels required for crafting pots and perfumes. The containers return when the item is used.

Cracked Pot

Crafting Container
Required for crafting throwable pot items
Empty pot that mends itself when broken. Maximum 20 can be held. Used for Fire Pots, Lightning Pots, Holy Water Pots, etc.
Reusable Various Sources

Ritual Pot

Crafting Container
Required for crafting ritual pot items
Larger pot for more powerful concoctions. Maximum 10 can be held. Used for Rancor Pot, Volcano Pot, and Alluring Pot.
Reusable Various Locations

Perfume Bottle

Crafting Container
Required for crafting perfume/aromatic items
Ornate bottle for perfume arts. Maximum 10 can be held. Used for Uplifting Aromatic, Spark Aromatic, and Ironjar Aromatic.
Reusable Perfumer Locations

Empty Ashes of War

Duplication Item
Used by Hewg to duplicate Ashes of War
Empty ashes devoid of battle memories. Smithing Master Hewg can use these to recreate other ashes. The copy becomes genuine.
Consumed Various Locations

Quest Items

Items tied to NPC questlines, offering lore significance and quest progression.

Deathroot

Beast Clergyman Quest
Trade to Gurranq for incantations/rewards
Source that gives rise to Those Who Live in Death. Gurranq at Bestial Sanctum collects and devours these roots. 9 available.
Given to NPC 9 Per Playthrough

Beast Eye

Tracking Item
Trembles when near Deathroot
Claw-marked stone eye from Gurranq. The murky violet iris writhes as if alive. "I am not sated... Feed me more... Death..."
Passive Detector Gurranq Gift

Chrysalid's Memento

Roderika Quest
Give to Roderika to progress her quest
Memento left by the chrysalids sacrificed for grafting. Found in Stormveil Castle. Allows Roderika to move to Roundtable Hold.
Given to NPC Stormveil Castle

Amber Starlight

Seluvis Quest
Give to Seluvis for Amber Draught
Large amber crystal with faint glow. Found on Altus Plateau. Required for Seluvis's potion-making schemes.
Given to NPC Altus Plateau

Seluvis's Potion

Choice Item
Can be given to Nepheli, Dung Eater, or Gideon
Cloudy tonic of bluish black. "Find Nepheli, and ensure she drinks it." Giving to different NPCs leads to different outcomes.
Given to NPC Multiple Outcomes

Weathered Dagger

Fia Quest
Return to its rightful owner (D)
Dagger from Fia that she wishes returned. Once gold and silver intertwined, now worn down and marred by a black gash.
Given to NPC Fia Quest

Fingerslayer Blade

Ranni Quest
Give to Ranni to progress her quest
Hidden treasure of Nokron; blade said to be born of a corpse. Required to advance Ranni's questline toward the Age of Stars.
Given to NPC Nokron, Eternal City

Carian Inverted Statue

Ranni Quest
Inverts Carian Study Hall
Given by Ranni after giving her the Fingerslayer Blade. Place on pedestal in Carian Study Hall to access Divine Tower of Liurnia.
Placed Once Ranni Gift

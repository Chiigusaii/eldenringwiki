COOKBOOKS

◆ ✧ ◆

Records of crafting techniques that expand your crafting repertoire. Collect cookbooks to unlock recipes for ammunition, consumables, pots, perfumes, and more. 104 cookbooks total—59 in the base game, 45 in Shadow of the Erdtree.

About Cookbooks

Cookbooks are Key Items that unlock new crafting recipes. You'll need the Crafting Kit from Merchant Kalé at the Church of Elleh (300 Runes) before you can craft anything. Cookbooks carry over to New Game+ but cannot be found again in subsequent journeys.

Some recipes require containers: Cracked Pots (max 20), Ritual Pots (max 10), or Perfume Bottles (max 10). These containers are reusable after the crafted item is consumed.

What is Elden Ring?

Elden Ring is an action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. Released on February 25, 2022, it is available on PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S, and PC.

The game was directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki, the legendary creator behind the Dark Souls series, Bloodborne, and Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice. In a historic collaboration, the game's mythos and worldbuilding were crafted in partnership with George R.R. Martin, the acclaimed author of "A Song of Ice and Fire" (Game of Thrones).

Set in the Lands Between, players take on the role of a Tarnished—an exile called back to the realm after the shattering of the Elden Ring. Your quest: to collect the Great Runes, defeat the demigod shardbearers, and ultimately become the Elden Lord.

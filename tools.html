TOOLS

◆ ✧ ◆

Reusable items that assist you throughout the Lands Between. From essential exploration gear like the Lantern and Telescope, to boss-weakening shackles and transformative dragon hearts. Unlike consumables, these tools are never depleted.

About Tools

Tools are reusable items that provide utility, damage, communication, or special effects. Unlike consumables, they are never used up and remain in your inventory permanently. Some require FP to use, while others are completely free.

Key tools like the Crafting Kit unlock gameplay features, while boss shackles can turn the tide of difficult encounters. The DLC adds transformation tools that let you take on dragon forms.

Essential Tools

Core tools for exploration, crafting, and traversal. Most are obtained early in your journey.

Crafting Kit

Key Item
Enables item crafting from main menu
Leather bag containing pestle, mortar, small blade, and tools. Essential for survival in the Lands Between.
300 Runes Reusable

Spectral Steed Whistle

Key Item
Summons Torrent, the spectral steed
Given by Melina when she offers her accord. Torrent is an invaluable companion for traversing the vast Lands Between.
Story Item Reusable

Memory of Grace

Starting Item
Return to last Site of Grace (lose all runes)
All Tarnished start with this. Useful when stuck, but costs all held runes. Cannot use during boss fights.
Starting Item Reusable

Lantern

Exploration Tool
Attach to waist to illuminate surroundings
Small waist-worn lantern. Dimmer than a torch but frees both hands for combat. Toggle on/off with use.
1,800 Runes No FP Cost

Telescope

Exploration Tool
See faraway things more clearly
Carian astrology tool. Part of a larger instrument, removed and stolen. Scout ahead for enemies and paths.
500 Runes No FP Cost

Tailoring Tools

Customization
Enables armor alterations at Sites of Grace
Portable set of tailoring tools. Remove capes, alter helms, and adjust armor appearance for 500 runes.
Coastal Cave Reusable

Golden Tailoring Tools

Customization

Elaborate tailoring tools once in the possession of Enia. Required to alter boss and demigod armor sets.

Spirit Calling Bell

Key Item
Enables Spirit Ash summoning
Given by Renna at Church of Elleh (night) or sold by Twin Maiden Husks. Required to summon Spirit Ashes.
NPC Gift Reusable

Boss Tools

Special items that weaken or stun specific bosses. Can only be used twice per fight, first phase only.

Margit's Shackle

Boss Tool
Binds Margit/Morgott to the ground briefly
Fetish bathed in golden magic. Stuns Margit and Morgott for a few seconds. Also reveals illusory walls and triggers dungeon traps.
5,000 Runes (Patches) 2x Per Fight

Mohg's Shackle

Boss Tool
Binds Mohg to the ground briefly
Works on both Mohg, the Omen and Mohg, Lord of Blood. Found deep in the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds, guarded by giant crayfish.
Shunning-Grounds 2x Per Fight

Blasphemous Claw

Boss Tool
Parry Maliketh's destined death attacks
Claw of the defeated Recusant Bernahl. Can parry Maliketh's golden blade attacks when timed correctly.
Bernahl Drop Reusable

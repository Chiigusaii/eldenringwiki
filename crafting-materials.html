CRAFTING MATERIALS

◆ ✧ ◆

Resources gathered throughout the Lands Between used to craft consumables, ammunition, and various items. Purchase a Crafting Kit from Merchant Kalé to unlock crafting.

About Crafting Materials

Materials can be found in the environment, dropped by enemies, or purchased from merchants. Most world materials respawn after resting at a Site of Grace. Item Discovery (Arcane stat, Silver Scarab talisman) increases drop rates from enemies.

Containers like Cracked Pots and Ritual Pots are reusable—when thrown items break, the container returns to your inventory.

REMEMBRANCES

◆ ✧ ◆

Powerful relics dropped by the mightiest bosses, hewn into the Erdtree and Scadutree. Each Remembrance can be exchanged with Finger Reader Enia at the Roundtable Hold for unique weapons, spells, or tools—or consumed for a great bounty of Runes. Duplicate at Walking Mausoleums to obtain both rewards.

How to Use Remembrances

1

Defeat a Boss

Obtain a Remembrance by defeating one of the 25 major bosses across the Lands Between and Land of Shadow.

2

Trade with Enia

Visit Finger Reader Enia at Roundtable Hold to exchange the Remembrance for one of two unique rewards.

3

Duplicate (Optional)

Use Walking Mausoleums to duplicate a Remembrance once, allowing you to claim both rewards in one playthrough.

4

Alternative: Consume

Consume the Remembrance directly for a large amount of Runes (not recommended for first playthrough).

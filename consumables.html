CONSUMABLES

◆ ✧ ◆

Expendable items for combat buffs, weapon coatings, throwing attacks, status cures, and more. Many can be crafted with a Crafting Kit and the appropriate Cookbooks. Stock up before challenging encounters!

About Consumables

Consumables are single-use items that provide temporary combat advantages. They range from attack buffs and weapon greases to throwing pots and status cures. Crafting is key to maintaining a healthy supply.

In PvP Colosseums, craftable consumables automatically replenish after each battle. Non-craftable items do not replenish.
